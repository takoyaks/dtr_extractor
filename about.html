About OTC Time Extractor

The OTC Time Extractor is a web-based tool created by DICT R5 Catanduanes to assist users in converting raw time logs from OpenTimeClock (OTC) into a structured Daily Time Record (DTR). This tool streamlines the preparation of DTRs for DICT employees by automating the transformation of raw time logs into an organized format, enhancing efficiency and minimizing errors for HR personnel managing attendance records.

The tool is designed to be user-friendly, allowing users to easily upload their raw time logs and download the formatted DTR. It is a valuable resource for HR personnel and employees alike, ensuring accurate and timely attendance records.

Due to limitations and lack of funds, weekends and holidays must be manually edited and merged.
