About OTC Time Extractor

The OTC Time Extractor is a tool developed by DICT R5 Catanduanes to help users process raw time logs from OpenTimeClock (OTC) into a formatted Daily Time Record (DTR). This tool simplifies the process of converting raw text into a structured table and allows users to download the data in CSV format.
